💡 How PTDT Staking Works

Real Yield Model: Staking rewards come from 40% of all protocol transaction fees. The other 60% is burned forever, making PTDT deflationary.

Three Tiers: Bronze (12% APY, 10 PTDT min), Silver (15% APY, 100 PTDT min), Gold (18% APY, 500 PTDT min). Higher tiers mean longer lock periods and better rewards.

On-Chain Settlement: PTDT serves as the infrastructure layer for global ride-hailing. Every ride generates fees, creating sustainable, non-inflationary yield for stakers.
