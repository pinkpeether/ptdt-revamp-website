General
05
What is Peether PTDT?
Peether PTDT is the blockchain token powering the Pink Taxi ecosystem. The concept began in 2006 as Pink Taxi and evolved into a Web3 settlement and utility layer for women-focused transportation.
What is the main objective of Peether PTDT?
The objective is to reduce harassment risk during commuting while creating secure transportation and economic opportunities for women.
Why is Peether PTDT needed?
Women in many markets still face serious safety issues in public transport and ride-hailing. PTDT connects that real-world need with verifiable settlement infrastructure and tokenized utility.
What makes Peether PTDT different from other ride-sharing platforms?
It combines Pink Taxi's women-focused operating history with blockchain transparency, protocol-level settlement, token utility, staking rewards, and a safety-first mission.
Is Pink Taxi only for women?
Yes. Pink Taxi is designed as a women-only service, with female drivers and female passengers creating a safer and more comfortable environment.
Token & Purchase
06
What is the Peether PTDT token symbol?
The token symbol is PTDT.
What are the benefits for token holders?
Token holders can participate in PTDT's ecosystem utility, token economy, staking rewards, ride-related benefits, and future operational incentives as the protocol expands.
How can I purchase PTDT tokens?
PTDT can be purchased through supported BNB Chain routes. A BEP-20 compatible wallet such as MetaMask or Trust Wallet is recommended. For assistance, contact support@ptdt.taxi.
What is the minimum and maximum purchase amount?
Minimum: 10 USDT. Maximum per transaction: 500 USDT.
When will Peether PTDT be listed on exchanges?
The exchange rollout starts with PancakeSwap on BNB Chain, followed by additional exchange targets.
What exchanges are you targeting?
PTDT is following a staged exchange approach beginning with PancakeSwap before wider exchange exposure.
Technical
02
What network is Peether PTDT on?
PTDT operates on BNB Smart Chain using the BEP-20 token standard.
What wallet should I use?
Use a personal BEP-20 compatible wallet where you control the private keys, such as MetaMask or Trust Wallet. Do not use an exchange wallet directly for token purchases or protocol interactions.
Operations & Expansion
04
Where does Pink Taxi operate?
Pink Taxi activity spans multiple countries including UAE, United States, India, Pakistan, Malaysia, Thailand, Australia, South Africa, Egypt, Turkey, Kuwait, Lebanon, Mexico, Iran, Russia, Armenia, and Mongolia.
What regions are priorities for expansion?
Priority regions include Southern and Latin America, African markets, European markets, and strengthened presence in existing Asian markets.
How does Pink Taxi empower women?
Pink Taxi creates safe earning opportunities for women drivers and safer transportation for female passengers, supporting participation in the economy with dignity and security.
Do drivers receive safety training?
Yes. The driver induction program includes self-defense and safety training delivered by female self-defense professionals at different skill levels.

02
How will token sale funds be used?
Token sale funds are allocated across IT and development, team, advisor and legal, reserve/treasury, marketing and bounty, community growth, private sale, liquidity pool, and referral program.
Is there a bounty program?
Yes. Participants supporting PTDT through social and promotional activity can earn PTDT through bounty campaigns announced through official channels.
